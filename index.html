Welcome to Snake - The Lion King Edition!

The game is simple: use the arrow keys to help move Simba across the grid to eat the food.

Try to make Simba eat as much as possible to gain points - the more he eats the bigger his pride gets. A word of caution, if you eat up to 12 foods then the speed will increase! Also be careful not to bump into your own body or the sides because IT WILL BE GAME OVER
